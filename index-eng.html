What is the "Flame of peace"





The Flamme des Friedens is an award given by the Austrian non-profit organization Vereins zur Förderung de Friedens (Association for the Promotion of Peace), which promotes peace around the world. The flame is a wood carving mounted on a stone base. The organization awards prizes to prominent people, world leaders and organizations for their work in promoting peace.







For the first time in Kyiv, the world-famous Ukrainian-Swedish pianist Natalia Pasichnyk performed the music of two Mozart musicians: Wolfgang Mozart and his son Frans Xaver Mozart, who lived and worked in Lviv for a long time.
