At the initiative of the Ambassador of Peace Anastasia Zelenskaya and with the participation of the Habsburg-Lothringen Royal Family from Austria, a monument "Flame of Peace" was unveiled in Kyiv.This monument is a reminder, as well as an incentive for all of us to act and think and promote peace on earth. This monument is made of wood (walnut tree - ed.) And stone, and both of these materials can be found on any continent of the world"- said the founder and president of the association" Flame of Peace " Ambassador of Peace, Her Imperial Highness Mrs. Herta Margaret Habsburg-Lothringen during the unveiling of the monument.After the grand opening of the monument, for the first time in Kyiv performed by the world-famous Ukrainian-Swedish pianist Natalia Pasichnyk together with the National Soloists Ensemble "Kyiv Chamber" The compositions of two Mozart were played: Wolfgang Mozart and his son Frans Xaver Mozart, who lived and worked in Ukraine, in Ukraine, for a long time of his life. 
The day after the unveiling of the "Flame of Peace" monument,the royal family of Habsburg-Lothringen and Anastasia Zelenska visited the orphanage "Malyatko" in Kyiv, where a children's playground was built with the support of the Flame of Peace organization.
Archbishop Damian thanks Zelenskaya A.L. for the icon of the Mother of God "Znamenya of the 12th century" brought as a gift to the Holy Vvedensky monastery.
Ceremony in memory of the NATO Memorial by Willie Breton. It is our duty not to forget that many NATO soldiers have sacrificed their lives for our security and freedom.This monument is made of the brick of the Kunduz Memorial (Afghanistan), a tribute to German soldiers killed in the Afghan theater, which was opened in Fretun. Since the end of World War II, the French army has participated in operations led by international organizations, primarily NATO and the UN. Willie Breton, a French soldier who served in the national gendarmerie, returned from a third round of Afghanistan in 2010, and wished to honor the memory of two fallen colleagues and all servicemen who died while serving under the NATO flag. A NATO Memorial was erected in Fretun on 25 February 2012.Guests of the ceremony: Anastasia Zelenskaya, Herta Margaret Habsburg Lorraine, Sandor Habsburg Lorraine, Verena Mayer "Flame of Peace Monaco", Fretun Mayor Catherine Fournier.
Annual reception of the organization "Flame of Peace" in Vienna, Anastasia Zelenskaya, singer Sherry Tyler and her husband, German artist P.O. Schabbach, who donated paintings to the Ukrainian organization "Flame of Peace", the proceeds from the sale of which went to help orphans.
